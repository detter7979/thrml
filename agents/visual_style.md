# thrml — Visual Style Guide

## Core Aesthetic
**"Residential Premium"** — High-end but unmistakably someone's backyard.
Not a spa. Not a hotel. A really well-kept private home.

## Must-Have Visual Elements
- **Cedar barrel saunas** (not prefab box saunas, not Finnish commercial)
- **Manicured residential lawns** — freshly cut, no weeds, believable Pacific Northwest yard
- **Natural wood tones** — cedar, teak, Douglas fir deck boards
- **Soft water features** — steam rising off a barrel sauna, cold plunge condensation
- **Real towels, real accessories** — one folded towel on a cedar bench,
  a water bottle, a wooden ladle — nothing overly styled

## Lighting References
| Time of Day | Mood | Use Case |
|---|---|---|
| Misty Morning | Soft diffused light, visible steam, blue-grey tones | Guest P1 prospecting |
| Golden Hour | Warm amber backlight, long shadows | Host acquisition |
| Blue Hour | Cool cobalt sky, warm interior glow from sauna | Retargeting / urgency |
| Overcast Noon | Even flat light, very clean — good for product detail | Static 1:1 format |

## Color Palette
- Background neutrals: warm whites, natural cedar, slate grey stone
- No artificial colour grading — no orange/teal cinema look
- Avoid: pure white backgrounds, dramatic vignettes, HDR processing

## Subject Positioning
- **Sauna always anchored** — never floating or cropped awkwardly
- Person (if present): back to camera or silhouetted — never a posed smile
- Empty space preferred for host creative (space is the product)
- One clear hero object per frame

## Hard Prohibitions (never generate)
- Fantasy or sci-fi lighting effects
- "Dramatic mossy" forest aesthetics
- Cinematic smoke machines
- Overstyled spa towel arrangements (rolled towels, rose petals)
- Commercial gym or hotel environments
- Swimming pools as primary subject
- Superhuman physiques or fitness-model aesthetics
- Snow or winter environments (off-brand for Seattle launch)
- Any AI-tell artefacts: extra fingers, melting edges, uncanny reflections

## Midjourney v7 Prompt Rules
- Write in natural language paragraphs — NO :: weight syntax
- Always specify: time of day, lighting quality, surface material, steam/condensation level
- Include: "shot on medium format film, slight grain" for realism signal
- Specify: "residential backyard, Seattle Pacific Northwest" for geo-grounding
- Avoid: "photorealistic," "hyperrealistic," "cinematic" — these trigger over-processing

## Format Specs by Placement
| Placement | Ratio | Composition |
|---|---|---|
| Feed / Stories | 9:16 | Sauna fills lower 60%, sky/steam upper 40% |
| Feed Static | 1:1 | Sauna centred, minimal headroom |
| Carousel card | 1:1 | Detail shot (door handle, ladle, steam vent) |
